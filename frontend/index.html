How It Works

Simple enough to feel like magic.

Four steps from purchase to a moment your recipient will carry with them.

01
🎁

Gift the bracelet

Choose a bracelet for someone you care about. It arrives with a unique NFC tag linked to their private portal.

02
✍️

Write your notes

You and anyone you invite can write notes anytime — birthdays, hard days, or just a quiet Tuesday morning.

03
📲

They tap to receive

When they tap their phone to the bracelet, the latest note appears instantly. No app needed. Just a moment.

04
📖

Notes live forever

Every note is archived. Years from now they'll have a collection of moments — and one day a printed keepsake.
